Mission Statement

Attention tree lovers, New Yorkers, and New York tree lovers! Did you know that we're currently in the midst of a once-in-a-decade event? NYC Parks is conducting the 2025 Tree Census, with the goal of counting every tree in the city. This is a massive undertaking that enlists thousands of volunteers to record the location, species, dimensions, and health of every tree across all five boroughs. According to the last tree census in 2015, New York City was home to over 800,000 trees.1
